## Decoration Themes
The game ships with over 100 decorative items grouped into themed sets. Decorations provide happiness, which grants coin bonuses on sales.

- **Country Classics:** Windmill, Wagon Wheel Fence, Flower Beds, Bird Bath.
- **Caribbean Cruise:** Palm Hammock, Rum Barrel Fountain, Beach Bonfire, Steel Drum Stage.
- **Harvest Festival:** Pumpkin Archway, Cornucopia Cart, Scarecrow Trio, Hay Bale Maze.
- **Winter Wonder:** Ice Carving, Snowy Gazebo, Gift-Laden Sleigh, Festive Market Stall.
- **Romantic Retreat:** Heart Topiary, Rose Pergola, Swan Pond, Love Seat Swing.
- **Tech Frontier:** Solar Array, Drone Landing Pad, Hydroponic Planter, Neon Signpost.

## Machine Bonuses
Farm machines grant passive boosts once constructed in the Machine Workshop.

- **Lucky Clover Harvester:** +5% chance for bonus crop yield.
- **Certificate Printer:** +10% certificates from events and contracts.
- **RP Amplifier:** +15% cooperative reputation from any co-op activity.
- **Auto-Feeder:** Reduces animal feed timer by 20% for 4 hours (consumable fuel).
- **Master Builder Crane:** Decreases building upgrade time by 15%.
